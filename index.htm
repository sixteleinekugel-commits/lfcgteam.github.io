// OUR MISSION

We are a team of students from Year 10 to Year 13 participating in FTC for the second year. Our goal is to build a reliable, high-performance robot while continuously learning and improving. After finishing 7th last year, we are now aiming for the podium.

// OUR ROBOT

IGGY 🐸

Our robot, Iggy, combines performance and identity. Inspired by a frog, it represents both our creativity and our French identity.

  • Ramp-based shooting system
  • Optimized trajectory for precision
  • Single controller driving
  • Autonomous phase implemented
