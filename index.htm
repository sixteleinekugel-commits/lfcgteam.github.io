// THE COMPETITION

The FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is an international robotics competition where teams design, build and program robots to compete in a dynamic game. It requires engineering, strategy, teamwork and problem-solving skills. Each year, teams must adapt to a new challenge, making innovation essential.

// OUR OBJECTIVES

Our objective this season was to build a reliable robot capable of completing all phases of the competition, including autonomous mode. After finishing 7th last year, we are aiming for the podium while improving our technical and teamwork skills.

// ENGINEERING PROCESS

We explored multiple design concepts including a swing system, a wheel launcher, and a ramp system. After testing and simulations, the ramp system was selected for its reliability and precision.
