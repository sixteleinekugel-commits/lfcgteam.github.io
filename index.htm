// THE LAB

Design Evolution

Initial Wheel Concept

We first explored passing the ball through a motorized wheel. However, 3D simulations revealed complications regarding speed control and the risk of the ball exiting the playing area.

The Swing Prototype

A creative idea using a mechanical pivot. While interesting, the time required to balance and test the catapult mechanism led us to prioritize a more reliable solution.

The Final Ramp Design

Our winning concept: a precisely angled ramp where a rotating rod propels the ball. This design offers the best balance of speed, accuracy, and mechanical repeatability.
