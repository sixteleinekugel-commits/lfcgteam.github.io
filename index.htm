// TECHNICAL DECISIONS & PROTOTYPES

Our design process is driven by 3D simulation and imagination. This year, we moved through three major concepts:

  • The Swing (Eliminated): Initially planned for ball propulsion, but ruled out as too complex to build reliably.
  • The Wheel Design (Eliminated): Dismissed due to potential speed issues and the risk of balls leaving the playing area.
  • The Optimized Ramp (Selected): A custom rod-propulsion system that prioritizes speed and accuracy.
