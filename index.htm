// OUR MISSION

We are an international team from Year 10 to Year 13. This year, we moved beyond basic designs to build a high-performance robot with an original look, avoiding standard competition templates.

After a tough first round where we missed the autonomy challenge, we made it our point of honor to master autonomous programming this season.

// DESIGN CHOICES

The Swing Concept

Initially, we planned a "swing" design for the balls. We pivoted to a ramp system after realizing the swing was too complex to maintain under pressure.

3D Simulation

Using 3D modeling, we eliminated two prototypes. One was too large, the other lacked the required projectile speed.
