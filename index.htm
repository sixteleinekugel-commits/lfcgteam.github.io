// ENGINEERING LAB

Design Evolution

V1: Motorized Centrifugation

Initial design utilized a high-speed orbital wheel. Simulation results showed 40% drift in ball trajectory. The system was abandoned to ensure maximum competitive accuracy.

> TRAJECTORY_STABILITY: FAILED
> POWER_OUTPUT: OVER_LIMIT
> DECISION: DECOMMISSIONED

V2: Geared Swing Mechanism

Used a lever-based catapult. While mechanically sound, the building time and footprint (18" limit) were too restrictive for the current season theme.

> SIZE_COMPLIANCE: WARNING
> COMPLEXITY: HIGH
> DECISION: ARCHIVED

V3: Precision Ramp & Rod

Current Active Protocol. Combines a fixed trajectory ramp with a rotating rod propulsion. Provides 95% repeatable scoring accuracy in both manual and autonomous modes.

> ACCURACY: 95%
> BUILD_RELIABILITY: OPTIMAL
> STATUS: ACTIVE_DEPLOYMENT
